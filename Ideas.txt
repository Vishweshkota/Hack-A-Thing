5. Worker Safety Wearable Band ⚠️
Problem: Industrial accidents cause injuries; workers in hazardous areas need monitoring.
Solution:

Fall detection via accelerometer
Panic button for emergencies
Wi-Fi SOS alerts to control room
Wi-Fi SSID-based location tracking indoors
Environmental sensors (gas, temperature)
Bluetooth proximity warnings near dangerous zones

Components Needed:

MPU6050 accelerometer/gyro
Push button
Optional: MQ gas sensor
